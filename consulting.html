Skype Consultations

I am available to provide assessment and consultancy services worldwide through Skype or other forms of online communication. Online consultations are as effective as face-to-face consultations and about half my consultations are carried out via online.

Assessments

This is a 45 minute session to assess the type and severity of the stutter. I will give immediate feedback and recommendations. A follow up report summarising findings will be provided within 2 weeks.
Assessment including report – SGD 150

Sessions

I adopt a multi-pronged approach in the sessions. I teach simple and practical techniques to help reduce stutter immediately, so that you can cope with everyday situations. At the same time, we will embark on a transformation therapy programme to change habitual patterns of thought and eradiate stutter. Each session is an hour.
One session – SGD 200
Eight sessions – SGD 1,500
