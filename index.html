Darren Teo

Stutter Specialist

Master of Art (Communication Study)

I am Darren and I help people who stutter. I adopt a multi-pronged approach in the sessions. I teach simple and practical techniques to help reduce stutter immediately, so that you can cope with everyday situations. At the same time, we will embark on a transformation therapy programme to change habitual patterns of thought and eradiate stutter. I am in Singapore but I offer consultations worldwide via Skype. I am happy to travel for full day consultancy or workshops. I believe I can help.
